# Meaning

Determination is the steady commitment to a goal, even in the face of obstacles or slow progress. It's the quiet strength that keeps you moving, despite setbacks, fatigue, or doubts. Unlike sheer willpower, which can feel intense and fleeting, determination is a deep, enduring force that grows stronger with every step you take, no matter how small. It teaches us that success isn't always about speed or immediate results; it's about resilience — staying true to your path and trusting that persistence will pay off over time.

# Question

What dreams or aspirations have you set aside because they seemed too difficult or too far away? How can you reconnect with your determination to move forward, even if the progress is slow and steady?

# Action

Choose one long-term goal that feels overwhelming, and break it down into smaller, manageable steps. For example, if you're working toward a major career change, rather than trying to overhaul everything at once, commit to researching one company, updating your resume, or scheduling one informational interview. Celebrate the small victories, and remember that the act of moving forward, no matter how slowly, is the very essence of determination.

# Quote

> "It does not matter how slowly you go as long as you do not stop."
> — Confucius
