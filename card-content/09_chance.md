# Meaning

Life doesn't always follow our plans. Sometimes, it surprises us — with change, with challenge, or with chance. Resilience is not just about getting through difficult moments, but about being open to what life offers, even when it looks unfamiliar or uncertain. Chance can feel like risk, but it can also be a door to something new — a shift in direction, an unexpected connection, or a deeper strength. When we meet the unknown not with fear but with flexibility and trust, we grow more resilient. We learn to move with life, not against it. Resilience is not only about holding steady — it's also about adapting, adjusting, and allowing ourselves to grow in response to what unfolds.

# Question

Can you remember a time when something uncertain or unexpected turned out better than you imagined? What would it feel like to stay open to life's surprises today — even the ones you can't yet understand?

# Action

Say yes to something small and uncertain today. Try a different route, talk to someone new, or take a step without knowing exactly where it leads. Notice how it feels to stay open. Resilience grows not only by holding firm, but also by bending when life invites us to shift.

# Quote

> "In the middle of every difficulty lies opportunity."
> — Albert Einstein
