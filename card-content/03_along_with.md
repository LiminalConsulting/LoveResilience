# Meaning

Resilience doesn't always have to be carried alone. Sometimes, strength comes from walking side by side — with someone, with a memory, with your values, or even with your past self. "Along with" reminds us that we're allowed to be held, accompanied, and supported. True resilience can mean accepting help, sharing the load, and remembering that connection can soften the path without weakening your strength.

# Question

Who or what walks alongside you right now — seen or unseen? How can you let that presence support your resilience today?

# Action

Take a quiet moment to acknowledge who or what has helped you stay strong. Maybe reach out to thank someone, or simply place a hand over your heart in gratitude for the presence that walks with you.

# Quote

> "If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together."
> — African proverb
