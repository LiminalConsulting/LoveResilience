# Meaning

Creativity is often seen as something for artists or musicians, but it's a vital part of all of us. It's the spark that allows us to imagine, to create, and to solve problems in new ways. As we grow older, we can sometimes lose touch with our creative energy — but resilience is the ability to reconnect with it. Nurturing creativity in our lives helps us adapt to challenges and approach life with fresh perspective and curiosity.

# Question

When was the last time you allowed yourself to be creative, without judgment or expectation? How can you give yourself permission to create freely today?

# Action

Find a moment today to engage in a creative activity that excites you — whether it's drawing, writing, crafting, or simply thinking outside the box. Allow yourself to create with no pressure or outcomes, just for the joy of the process.

# Quote

> "Creativity is intelligence having fun."
> — Albert Einstein
