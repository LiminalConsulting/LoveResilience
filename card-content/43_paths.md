# Meaning

Sometimes we wonder if we've taken the right path — if a choice made long ago has led us closer to ourselves, or further away. Life rarely offers immediate clarity. What feels like a mistake may later become the turning point. What begins as loss may open into a freedom we hadn't imagined. The road shifts — sometimes with intention, sometimes by force — and we're left to walk it without knowing where it will lead. And yet... with time, we begin to see how even the unexpected detours shape something essential in us. The real strength isn't always in knowing, but in continuing — with an open heart, with presence, and with the quiet courage to walk into the unknown. Some paths reveal their meaning only once we've lived them.

# Question

Can you think of a moment that felt like a wrong turn at the time, but offered you something meaningful in the end? Is there a part of your path right now that you're still learning to trust?

# Action

Sit quietly and recall a turning point in your life — a decision, a shift, or an unexpected event. What did it ask of you? What did it teach you? Write a short letter to your past self, standing at that crossroads. Let them know what grew from that moment.

Then, if you're facing uncertainty now, whisper to yourself:

Even when I can't see the whole path, I trust that each step carries meaning.
