# Meaning

Faith is the quiet inner knowing that there is light, even when we cannot yet see it. It's not tied to certainty or answers — it's a deep trust in life, in the unfolding of each moment, and in our capacity to meet whatever comes. Sometimes, resilience depends on this kind of trust. Without it, we freeze, resist, or give up. Faith helps us keep going — not because we are sure of the outcome, but because we believe in the journey itself. It reminds us that we are held by something greater than our fears — whether that's life, love, or the quiet strength within.

# Question

When you feel lost or uncertain, what helps you keep believing? Can you connect with a sense of inner trust, even if just a flicker?

# Action

Choose something in your life right now that feels uncertain, heavy, or unclear. Instead of trying to solve or fix it, pause. Sit quietly, breathe, and gently ask yourself: What do I need to trust right now? Let the answer come slowly, if at all. Then, write down one small, meaningful act that reflects that trust — it might be making a call, letting go of a task, resting without guilt, or continuing a commitment even in doubt. Honor that act as a sign of resilience — not because you know where things are going, but because you're still choosing to move forward with an open heart.

# Quote

> "Faith is the bird that feels the light and sings when the dawn is still dark."
> — Rabindranath Tagore
