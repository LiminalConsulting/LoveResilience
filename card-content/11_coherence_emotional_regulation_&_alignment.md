# Meaning

Coherence, in scientific terms, refers to the harmonious alignment between your heart, mind, and emotions. When we experience emotional coherence, our heart rhythm becomes smooth and consistent, with a pattern that's balanced and steady. This happens when our emotions, thoughts, and physiological responses are in sync, often leading to greater emotional clarity, a calmer nervous system, and improved decision-making. In this state, we access a deeper form of resilience, because we're no longer fighting against ourselves. Instead, we become more grounded in the present, which helps us respond to life's challenges with clarity and inner strength. Coherence doesn't mean everything is perfect — it means there's a natural flow and balance that allows us to stay grounded, even in turbulence.

# Question

Can you recall a time when everything seemed to fall into place, even if just for a moment? What might help you tap into that state of coherence today?

# Action

Place your hand over your heart. Let your attention rest there. Simply feel whatever is present — without needing to change it.
