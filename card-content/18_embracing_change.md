# Meaning

Change is inevitable, but our response to it is what shapes our resilience. True strength isn't about holding onto the past or resisting transformation — it's about being flexible, open, and willing to grow through change. Resilience is found in our ability to adapt, adjust, and evolve when life presents new circumstances. Just like in nature, survival and growth are tied to our responsiveness to change. The more we embrace it, the more we build our inner strength and resilience. By responding with curiosity and openness, we unlock new possibilities and find deeper layers of our own power.

# Question

What change are you currently facing that could become an opportunity for growth? How might your response to this change reveal your inner resilience?

# Action

Identify one area of your life where change is present — whether it's an external shift or an internal adjustment. Today, take one action to embrace this change. It could be as simple as changing your perspective, seeking something positive in the change, or taking a small step forward in adapting to it. In welcoming change, you build your resilience.
