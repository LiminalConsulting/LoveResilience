# Meaning

We often imagine resilience as toughness — pushing through, standing firm.

But true resilience isn't rigid. It bends without breaking.

And that's where tenderness comes in.

To stay soft in a hard world.

To keep your heart open after disappointment.

To feel deeply and still move forward — that is resilience at its most courageous.

Resilience is not the absence of emotion.

It's the willingness to feel — and still choose hope, healing, and action.
