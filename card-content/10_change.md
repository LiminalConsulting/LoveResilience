# Meaning

Change is one of life's constants — sometimes welcome, sometimes unsettling. It can disrupt what we know, shift our footing, and ask us to let go of what once felt safe. Resilience doesn't mean resisting change or pretending it's easy; it means learning how to stay steady and responsive when everything around us is moving. True resilience grows when we meet change not with fear, but with curiosity, courage, and a willingness to evolve. In change, we are invited to not only adapt, but to reconnect with who we are becoming.

# Question

How do you usually respond to change? Can you think of a time when change brought something meaningful or unexpected into your life?

# Action

The next time something changes — big or small — pause for a moment instead of reacting right away. Take a breath and ask yourself: What is this moment asking of me? What do I need to let go of, and what might be waiting on the other side?

# Quote

> "The only thing that is constant is change."
> — Heraclitus
