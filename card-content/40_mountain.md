# Meaning

Mountains do not rise without earthquakes. What looks solid and immovable from a distance has been shaped by chaos, pressure, and time. Your strength may feel quiet now, but it came from movement — from surviving the shaking ground and choosing to keep standing. Just like a mountain, your foundation may have been formed in struggle, but your presence is steady, rooted, and unmistakably real.

The truth is: your depth came from what cracked you open. You're not defined by what happened to you, but by what you found within yourself as it happened. Stillness doesn't mean nothing is happening. Often, it means transformation is working deep below the surface.

# Question

What part of your story made you who you are — not in spite of the challenge, but because of it? Can you honour that today, instead of hiding it?

# Action

Stand tall — literally. Feel your feet planted on the ground and imagine the weight of your life becoming your roots. Inhale as though drawing up strength from the earth. Exhale into calm, solid presence. Whisper to yourself: "I have been shaped, not shattered." Carry that with you.

# Quote

> "Now, everytime I witness a strong person, I want to know: What dark did you conquer in your story? Mountains do not rise without earthquakes."
> — Katherine Mackenett
