# Meaning

Sometimes resilience doesn't look like action — it sounds like a melody you've listened to on repeat, a rhythm that holds your breath steady, or a song that understands you better than words. Music can shift your state, soften what's heavy, or give shape to what you couldn't quite name. It's not about fixing anything — it's about feeling your way through. Let music hold what you can't carry alone.

# Question

What kind of music meets you where you are today? What sound would support your nervous system, your heart, or the version of you that needs a little company?

# Action

Put on a piece of music that matches or gently shifts your emotional state. Let yourself listen fully — no multitasking, no goal. Just let it move through you.

# Quote

> "Music expresses that which cannot be said and on which it is impossible to be silent."
> — Victor Hugo
