# Meaning

Being courageous isn't about being fearless; it's about embracing your fears and still choosing to move forward. Courage is the quality that allows us to take risks, speak up, or step into uncertain situations — knowing that resilience is born from overcoming what feels intimidating. It's through courage that we build strength, grow, and discover what we're truly capable of.

# Question

What fear is holding you back right now? How can you take one courageous step toward facing it today, no matter how small?

# Action

Identify one fear or challenge that has been lingering for you. Take a small, courageous action towards it today — whether that's having a difficult conversation, trying something new, or simply taking a step outside your comfort zone. Celebrate that you moved forward despite the fear.

# Quote

> "Courage is resistance to fear, mastery of fear – not absence of fear."
> — Mark Twain
