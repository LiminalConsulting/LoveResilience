# Meaning

Resilience is rooted in the ability to trust the quiet wisdom that comes from within. Intuition is the inner voice that gently points the way, even when the path ahead is unclear. It doesn't need explanation; it simply knows. Resilience isn't about having all the answers, but about being open to the guidance that arises from your deepest self. When you listen to that wisdom and trust the feeling it brings, you begin to move through life with a quiet strength that doesn't need to be forced.

# Question

When was the last time you followed your intuition, even if it didn't make sense at the time? What did it feel like to trust it?

# Action

Find a quiet space and close your eyes. Take a few deep breaths and tune in to your body. Pay attention to any sensations you feel — a flutter, a warmth, or even goosebumps. These are often signs that your intuition is guiding you. Don't analyze or judge the feeling, just let it exist. Allow yourself to follow what feels right in the moment.

Training your intuition is a practice that requires patience, trust, and consistent effort. Over time, you'll begin to notice that your intuitive insights become stronger, clearer, and easier to act on. Start by following small nudges, like taking a different route or calling someone you feel compelled to reach out to. Trusting these little moments helps strengthen your connection to your intuition and enhances your resilience in navigating life's challenges.

# Quote

> "Intuition is a spiritual faculty and does not explain, but simply points the way."
> — Florence Scovel Shinn
