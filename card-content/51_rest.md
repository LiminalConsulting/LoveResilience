# Meaning

Resilience isn't about pushing through every challenge without pause. It's about knowing when to rest, so that you can come back with renewed strength.

In our fast-paced world, rest is often overlooked. But it's in those quiet moments of stillness — the deep breath, the gentle pause — that true restoration happens.

Rest doesn't mean giving up. It means gathering the energy needed to continue with even more clarity and purpose.

# Question

Today, how can I honor rest as a necessary part of my resilience?

# Action

Give yourself permission to pause — even if just for the length of an exhale — and notice what softens when you do.

# Quote

> "Sometimes the most productive thing you can do is relax."
> — Mark Black
