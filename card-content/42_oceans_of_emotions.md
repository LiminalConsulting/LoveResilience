# Meaning

Emotions can come in waves — sometimes soft, sometimes overwhelming. They aren't wrong or too much; they just ask to be felt and witnessed. Resilience doesn't mean holding them back — it means learning to float, to pause, to not be swept away. In that space between feeling and reacting lies your power. That's where your choice lives. Your strength isn't in silencing the emotion, but in how you meet it.

# Question

Can you give yourself a little more space today — between what you feel and what you do next? What does that pause need in order to feel safe?

# Action

When a strong feeling rises, see if you can breathe into it instead of away from it. Give it a name. Let it exist without judgment. And then, gently, decide how you want to respond — not react.

# Quote

> "Between stimulus and response, there is a space... In that space is our power to choose our response."
> — Viktor E. Frankl
