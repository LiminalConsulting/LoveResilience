# Meaning

Resilience flourishes when we allow others to walk beside us — not to carry us, but to share the weight of the journey.

Quality time with someone special is not about escaping challenges, but facing them together, with presence and understanding.

In those quiet moments, we don't have to be perfect; we just have to be real. With shared vulnerability and deep connection, we find strength we didn't know we had.

Together, we become more than the sum of our struggles.

# Question

Who in my life walks beside me in a way that strengthens both of us? How can we create more space for shared, meaningful moments?
