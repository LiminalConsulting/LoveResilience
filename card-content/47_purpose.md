# Meaning

Purpose often feels like a big idea, but it can start with small steps. It's about finding what brings meaning to your life — whether it's something you love, something you're good at, or something the world needs or something you can be rewarded for.

# Question

What's one thing today that connects you to something meaningful? It could be something small, like a task that aligns with your values or an act that brings joy to someone else.

# Action

Take one small step toward that purpose today — even if it's just a simple action, like reaching out to someone, completing a small task, or spending a moment reflecting on what matters most.

# Quote

> "The meaning of life is to find your gift. The purpose of life is to give it away."
> — Pablo Picasso
