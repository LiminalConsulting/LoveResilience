# Meaning

This image of the lotus has always stayed with me — rising not in spite of the mud, but because of it. It's such a gentle reminder that we don't have to wait for perfect conditions to grow.

Even in the messiness, the heaviness, there is movement. Quiet blooming. And maybe, like the lotus, we aren't meant to stay in the darkness forever — but to take something from it and let it shape our light.

# Question

What part of you is quietly growing beneath the surface, even in the mess — ready, one day, to bloom?

# Action

Visualize a lotus rising slowly through murky water — and let one part of yourself rise with it, without rushing.

# Quote

> "Just like the lotus we too have the ability to rise from the mud, bloom out of the darkness and radiate into the world."
> — Gautama Buddha
